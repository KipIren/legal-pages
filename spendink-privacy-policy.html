PRIVACY POLICY FOR SPENDINK EXPENSE TRACKER

This Privacy Policy explains how SpendInk ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

1. OVERVIEW

SpendInk is designed to respect and protect user privacy. The application operates primarily through local device storage and does not require user accounts or cloud-based data storage. We aim to be transparent about how information is handled within the app and through integrated third-party services.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 FINANCIAL DATA (STORED LOCALLY ON YOUR DEVICE)

  • Expense and income entries
  • Account balance and related calculations
  • Custom categories
  • Savings and budgeting information
  • Recurring transaction settings
  • Currency conversion selections
  • Budget configurations

2.2 USER PREFERENCES (STORED LOCALLY ON YOUR DEVICE)

  • Language selection
  • Theme and appearance settings
  • Preferred currency
  • Notification settings
  • Quick-action configurations
  • Subscription status

2.3 SECURITY SETTINGS (STORED LOCALLY ON YOUR DEVICE)

  • Encrypted passcode data
  • Biometric authentication preferences
  • App access protection settings

2.4 INFORMATION COLLECTED BY THIRD PARTIES

The app uses third-party services that may collect limited information necessary for their functionality.

a) Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

  • Device identifiers
  • General device information
  • Ad performance data

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

b) Exchange Rate API

  • Currency codes for rate retrieval

No personally identifiable information is shared.

c) Apple In-App Purchases

  • Information required to process transactions

Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

3.1 FINANCIAL DATA

  • Display and manage financial entries
  • Generate summaries, insights, and analytics
  • Support savings and budgeting features
  • Process recurring transactions
  • Provide currency conversion functionality

3.2 USER PREFERENCES

  • Customize app appearance and behavior
  • Deliver reminder notifications
  • Preserve app settings across updates

3.3 SECURITY SETTINGS

  • Protect access to the application
  • Verify authentication using passcode or biometrics

3.4 THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

  • Deliver advertisements
  • Retrieve currency exchange information
  • Process in-app purchases

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

4.1 LOCAL STORAGE

  • All financial and preference data is stored on your device
  • Information is saved using secure mechanisms provided by the operating system
  • Access protections rely on your device's security features

4.2 NO CLOUD STORAGE

  • We do not maintain servers for storing personal or financial information
  • Data is not uploaded or synced to external services except where required by third-party integrations

4.3 SECURITY MEASURES

  • Optional passcode protection
  • Optional biometric authentication
  • OS-level sandboxing and data isolation
  • Secure purchase processing via platform providers

5. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

5.1 GENERAL POLICY

We do not sell or trade personal information. Financial data remains stored on your device.

5.2 LIMITED SHARING

Information shared with third parties is limited to:

  • Advertising and device data for AdMob
  • Currency codes for exchange rate retrieval
  • Transaction details for in-app purchases

5.3 LEGAL REQUIREMENTS

We may disclose information if required to comply with legal obligations, though the app retains minimal data that could be subject to such requests.

5.4 USER-INITIATED SHARING

The app allows you to export your data. Any sharing of exported files is fully controlled by you.
